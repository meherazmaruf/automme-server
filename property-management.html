Why property managers struggle to scale.

Most property management companies lose revenue and waste hours because their operations run on spreadsheets, sticky notes, and disjointed software. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Vacancy bleed

Every day a unit sits empty, you lose money. Without automated tenant sourcing, show scheduling, and application follow-up, vacancies stretch for weeks longer than they should. Our AI fills units faster by automating the entire leasing pipeline.

Maintenance chaos

Tenant calls, texts, emails, portal tickets — maintenance requests come from everywhere but get lost everywhere. Without a centralized dispatch AI, emergency repairs go unanswered, routine maintenance gets delayed, and tenant satisfaction plummets.

Rent collection gaps

Late payments, forgotten due dates, manual follow-up — chasing rent eats hours every month. Without automated reminders, late fee assessment, and payment plan negotiation, your cash flow suffers and collections become a part-time job.

Lease renewal leakage

Good tenants move out simply because nobody asked them to stay. Without automated renewal campaigns timed at 90, 60, and 30 days before lease end, you lose reliable tenants to competitors who actually follow up.

Tenant lead gen AI

Maintenance dispatch automation
AI triages every maintenance request — categorizes urgency, assigns the right vendor or in-house tech, and provides real-time updates to tenants. Emergency after-hours dispatch, routine scheduling, and vendor performance tracking. One system, zero dropped requests.
Rent collection automation
Automated rent reminders via SMS and email, late fee assessment, payment plan setup, and eviction filing triggers. AI predicts which tenants are at risk of late payment and proactively reaches out with payment options before the due date passes.
Lease renewal campaigns

Tenant screening AI
Automated tenant screening that checks credit, criminal, eviction history, income verification, and landlord references in minutes. AI scores applicants and recommends approval, deposit amount, or denial based on your custom criteria. Consistent, fair, fast.
Portfolio reporting dashboards

AI-coordinated vendor network for maintenance, repairs, landscaping, and snow removal. Automated bid requests, scheduling, invoice processing, and performance scoring. Reduce vendor response time and negotiate better rates with data-driven leverage.
Tenant communication AI
Centralized AI tenant communication hub — SMS, email, and voice for rent reminders, maintenance updates, lease renewal offers, and community announcements. Multi-language support, personalized messaging, and full conversation history per tenant.
