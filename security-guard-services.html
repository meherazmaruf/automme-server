Why security guard companies struggle to scale.

Most security companies lose margin to scheduling inefficiency, high turnover, manual billing, and inconsistent client acquisition. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Scheduling chaos

Managing guard schedules across multiple sites, shifts, and client requirements is a nightmare. Without AI scheduling, call-outs create gaps, overtime spikes, and clients get angry when posts are left uncovered. Our AI schedules perfectly every time.

Client acquisition inconsistency

Winning new security contracts requires consistent outreach to facility managers, property managers, and security directors. Without automated client prospecting, your sales team wastes time on cold outreach and loses bids to competitors.

Incident reporting delays

Guards fill out paper incident reports that sit in binders for weeks. Without AI-powered digital incident reporting, you miss critical information, delay client notifications, and expose your company to liability.

Billing & payroll complexity

Security companies bill by the hour across multiple sites with different rates, overtime rules, and contract terms. Without automated billing and payroll integration, errors are common and cash flow suffers.
