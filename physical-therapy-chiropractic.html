Why PT & chiropractic practices struggle to grow.

Most therapy practices lose patients to slow intake, insurance confusion, poor attendance, and no-show rates that crush revenue. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Intake paperwork friction

New patients spend 20+ minutes filling out paper forms in your waiting room. Without digital intake automation, you lose impatient prospects, staff wastes hours on data entry, and the patient experience starts with frustration instead of care.

Insurance verification delays

Calling insurance companies to verify benefits takes 15-20 minutes per patient, and eligibility changes constantly. Without automated insurance verification, your front desk spends more time on the phone than with patients, and claim denials pile up.

No-show epidemic

PT and chiro practices average 15-25% no-show rates. Missed appointments mean lost revenue, wasted clinician time, and longer wait times for other patients. Without automated reminder and rebooking systems, every empty slot is money you never recover.

Patient drop-off after initial visit

Many patients come for 1-2 visits and never return, even with a valid treatment plan. Without automated engagement and re-activation campaigns, you lose patients who needed ongoing care simply because nobody followed up.

Everything your PT & chiropractic practice needs.

A complete AI-powered growth and operations stack for physical therapy and chiropractic. From first inquiry to ongoing care, every system works together to maximize patient visits per week.

Patient intake AI
Digital intake forms, automated patient registration, consent collection, and health history capture via web and mobile. AI pre-populates forms from past visits and identifies key health signals for the clinician. Cut intake time from 20 minutes to 2 minutes.
Insurance verification automation
AI that automatically verifies patient insurance benefits before the first visit — eligibility, copays, deductibles, visit limits, and pre-authorization requirements. Automated patient notification of coverage details. Reduce front desk verification time by 80%.
Appointment booking & scheduling
24/7 online booking with automated scheduling logic — matches patients with the right clinician based on condition, availability, and insurance. Confirmations, reminders, and waitlist management. Fill cancellations instantly from a priority waitlist.
No-show reduction system
Multi-channel appointment reminders — SMS, email, and voice calls at 72h, 48h, 24h, and 2h before appointment. AI identifies high-risk no-show patients and triggers extra reminders or offers same-day incentives. Reduce no-shows from 20% to under 5%.
Patient re-activation campaigns
Automated sequences targeting patients who stopped coming — post-visit check-ins, outcome surveys, re-assessment offers, and seasonal promotions. AI identifies patients who would benefit from returning and sends personalized re-engagement messages.
Patient portal & home exercise AI
White-label patient portal with home exercise programs, progress tracking, and secure messaging. AI generates personalized exercise plans based on diagnosis and monitors compliance through patient check-ins and video form review.
Referral generation AI
Automated referral campaigns targeting physicians, orthopedists, pain management specialists, and wellness centers. AI identifies referring provider patterns and nurtures relationships with personalized outreach. Turn every referring doctor into a reliable pipeline.
Review & reputation management
Automated post-visit review requests — happy patients go to Google, review platforms get positive reviews. AI monitors all review platforms, flags negative reviews within minutes, and triggers internal alerts for immediate response. Build a 5-star online reputation.
Practice analytics dashboard
Real-time practice metrics — visits per week, no-show rate, revenue per clinician, patient acquisition cost, and visit utilization. AI identifies trends and recommends staffing adjustments, marketing spend changes, and scheduling improvements.
Billing & collections AI
Automated claim submission, payment posting, denial management, and patient billing. AI identifies billing errors before submission, tracks claim status, and follows up on denials automatically. Reduce days in A/R and increase collection rates.
Patient satisfaction tracking
Automated outcome measurement — surveys at intake, discharge, and follow-up intervals. AI tracks functional outcome scores, pain levels, and patient satisfaction. Clinicians get real-time feedback on treatment effectiveness and patient progress.
