Why restaurants struggle to grow.

Most restaurants leave revenue on the table because their operations and marketing run on fragmented, manual systems. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Reservation management chaos

Managing reservations across phone calls, online booking, walk-ins, and third-party platforms creates constant overbooking, long wait times, and frustrated guests. Without AI-driven table management, you're either turning away customers or leaving seats empty.

Online ordering friction

Clunky online ordering systems, high third-party delivery fees, and abandoned carts drain direct revenue. Most restaurants lose 20-30% of potential online orders due to poor UX, slow checkout, or confusing menu navigation that drives customers to competitors.

Review response overload

Managing Google, Yelp, TripAdvisor, and social media reviews is a full-time job. Negative reviews go unanswered, positive ones go un-thanked, and you miss the chance to turn guest feedback into marketing fuel. Reputation drifts without a system.

Staff scheduling nightmares

Manual shift scheduling, last-minute call-outs, and overstaffing during slow periods destroy your margins. Without AI-driven scheduling based on reservation forecasts and historical traffic patterns, labor costs eat into already thin restaurant margins.
