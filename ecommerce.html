Why e-commerce brands leak revenue.

Most online stores leave money on the table because their marketing and operations run on disconnected, manual systems. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Cart abandonment draining revenue

E-commerce brands lose nearly 70% of shoppers before they complete a purchase. Without intelligent follow-up sequences and real-time incentives, that revenue walks away forever. Our AI recovers abandoned carts with personalized offers and timing.

Inventory blind spots

Stockouts and overstock both kill margins. Most stores rely on gut feelings or spreadsheets for inventory decisions. Our AI forecasts demand by SKU, season, and channel — so you always have the right stock at the right time.

Customer support doesn't scale

As order volume grows, support tickets pile up. Response times stretch, customer satisfaction drops, and you need to hire more agents. AI handles 80% of inquiries instantly — order status, returns, shipping questions — without adding headcount.

Email marketing that doesn't convert

Blast-and-forget email campaigns are dead. Without behavioral triggers, dynamic segmentation, and personalized product recommendations, your emails land in spam or get ignored. Our AI sends the right message to the right person at the right time.
