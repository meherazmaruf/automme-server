Why HR & recruiting teams struggle to scale.

Most HR teams leave great hires on the table because their processes run on manual, time-consuming systems. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Resume screening overload

Recruiters spend 70% of their time screening resumes that don't match. For a single role, you might review 200+ applications to find 5 qualified candidates. AI can screen thousands of resumes in seconds and rank them by fit — freeing your team to focus on the best candidates.

Candidate outreach is manual

Passive candidate outreach requires personalized messaging at scale — but most recruiters copy-paste generic InMails that get ignored. AI crafts personalized outreach based on each candidate's profile, experience, and interests — dramatically improving response rates.

Interview scheduling chaos

Coordinating calendars between hiring managers, interviewers, and candidates creates endless email chains and scheduling delays. Every day of delay means your top candidates accept other offers. Automated scheduling cuts time-to-interview from days to minutes.

Onboarding paperwork nightmare

New hire paperwork — offer letters, I-9s, tax forms, benefit enrollment — is still handled manually at most companies. Errors, delays, and lost documents create a poor first impression and expose you to compliance risk. Automated onboarding eliminates the friction.
