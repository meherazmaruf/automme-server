Why law firms struggle to grow.

Most legal practices leave cases on the table because their intake and operations run on outdated, manual processes. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Client intake inefficiency

Potential clients call, email, and fill web forms — but without automated intake, these leads pile up in inboxes and voicemail. By the time someone follows up, the prospect has already called three other firms. Our AI captures and qualifies every lead instantly.

Case qualification takes too long

Paralegals spend hours on initial case assessments, reviewing facts against practice areas, and determining viability. This manual bottleneck means slow response times and inconsistent qualification criteria across your team.

Document overload

Legal document preparation, discovery review, and contract analysis consume billable hours that could go to higher-value work. Without AI document automation, your team is buried in paperwork instead of practicing law.

Missed calls = missed cases

Law firms lose 30%+ of new client opportunities because calls go unanswered after hours or during court appearances. Every missed call is a potential six-figure case walking out the door. Our AI answers and qualifies 24/7.

Everything your law firm needs.

A complete AI-powered growth and operations stack built for legal. From the first client call to case resolution, every system works together to maximize your caseload and minimize overhead.

Client intake AI
Multi-channel intake automation that captures leads from phone calls, web forms, social media, and chat. AI qualifies prospects, captures case details, and routes high-value leads to the right attorney instantly. Zero leads fall through the cracks.
Case qualification chatbots
Conversational AI that pre-qualifies potential clients through a structured intake interview. Collects case facts, practice area fit, and urgency — then assigns a qualification score and routes to the right team member for immediate follow-up.
Document automation
AI-powered document generation for contracts, pleadings, discovery requests, and client correspondence. Templates populated automatically from intake data. Reduces document preparation time by 70% while eliminating manual errors.
Consult booking automation
Automated consultation scheduling that syncs with attorney calendars. Prospects book initial consults through your website, Google Business Profile, or SMS — with automatic confirmation, reminder, and rescheduling workflows.
CRM automation
Every lead and client interaction automatically logged and scored in your CRM. Automated follow-up sequences for unqualified leads, past clients, and referral sources. No manual data entry, no missed touchpoints.
Review generation
Automated review request campaigns that route satisfied clients to Google, Avvo, and Martindale-Hubbell. Steady review velocity improves local SEO rankings and builds the social proof that drives legal client decisions.
Settlement calculator
Interactive settlement value calculator that prospects can use on your website. AI crunches case variables to provide estimated ranges. Captures lead information and triggers immediate follow-up from the appropriate practice area team.
Referral tracking
Automated referral source tracking and nurture campaigns. Identify which attorneys, past clients, and partners send you the best cases. Reward top referrers and systematically expand your referral network with AI-driven outreach.
Email nurturing
Automated email sequences for lead nurturing, case updates, and cross-practice area marketing. AI segments contacts by practice area interest and case stage, delivering relevant content that converts prospects into retained clients.
Compliance automation
Automated compliance workflows for client intake disclosures, conflict checks, and document retention. AI ensures every new matter follows your firm's ethical and regulatory requirements — reducing risk and administrative overhead.
