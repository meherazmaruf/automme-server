Why fitness studios struggle to grow.

Most gyms and studios fight a constant battle against churn, empty classes, and slow lead conversion. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Membership retention is a leaky bucket

Fitness studios lose 30-50% of members within the first six months. Without automated engagement and at-risk detection, you don't know who's about to cancel until they're already gone. Our AI identifies at-risk members and triggers retention sequences before they churn.

Class booking friction kills revenue

Complicated booking interfaces, forgotten classes, and last-minute cancellations leave half-empty classes and wasted capacity. Automated booking reminders, waitlist management, and class utilization analytics fill every session to capacity.

Lead-to-trial conversion is broken

Free trials and intro offers generate leads, but without automated follow-up, most never convert to paid memberships. Our AI nurture sequences guide trial members through the journey from first visit to first paid month.

Seasonal churn hits like clockwork

New Year's resolutions fade, summer vacations empty studios, and holiday schedules break habits. Without automated seasonal campaigns, you lose members you could have kept. AI-driven re-engagement campaigns prevent seasonal drop-off.
