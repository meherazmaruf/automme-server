Why auto dealerships struggle to grow.

Most dealerships leave sales on the table because their lead management and operations run on fragmented, slow systems. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Lead response speed

Studies show that dealers who respond to leads within 5 minutes are 100x more likely to convert. Yet most dealerships take hours or days to follow up on web inquiries, chat messages, and phone calls. Every minute of delay is a lost sale to a faster competitor.

Test drive scheduling friction

Customers want to book test drives instantly — but most dealerships require phone tag, back-and-forth emails, or in-person visits to schedule. This friction causes 30%+ of interested buyers to move on to a dealer with seamless online booking.

Inventory management blind spots

Without real-time inventory visibility across lots and locations, dealers overstock slow movers while running out of high-demand models. AI-powered demand forecasting can optimize inventory turns and reduce carrying costs significantly.

Follow-up fatigue

Sales teams start strong but fade fast. Leads that don't buy in the first week get forgotten — even though 80% of car buyers purchase within 30 days of their first inquiry. Without automated follow-up, your team leaves thousands in commissions on the table.

Everything your dealership needs.

A complete AI-powered sales and marketing stack built for auto dealerships. From the first lead to the final sale, every system works together to move metal faster and maximize gross profit.

Lead response AI
Instant lead response system that replies to web inquiries, chat messages, and phone calls within seconds. AI qualifies buyers, answers vehicle questions, captures contact details, and schedules test drives — all before your sales team picks up the phone.
Test drive booking
Online test drive scheduling that integrates with your inventory and sales calendar. Customers book specific vehicles for specific time slots — with automatic confirmation, reminders, and rescheduling. Reduces no-show rates and maximizes demo slot utilization.
Inventory alerts
Real-time inventory monitoring with AI-driven demand forecasting. Automatic alerts when stock runs low on high-demand models, plus recommendations for pricing adjustments and trade-in acquisitions based on market trends and local demand patterns.
Re-engagement campaigns
Automated re-engagement sequences for leads that went cold, past customers due for trade-in, and service customers ready to upgrade. AI identifies the optimal timing and offer for each contact, then triggers personalized multi-channel campaigns.
CRM automation
Every lead and customer interaction automatically logged and scored. AI assigns leads to the right salesperson based on buyer type, vehicle interest, and proximity. Automated workflows for test drive follow-up, proposal delivery, and post-sale handoff to service.
Review generation
Automated review request campaigns that route happy buyers to Google, Cars.com, and DealerRater. Smart timing ensures requests go out at peak satisfaction moments. Steady review velocity builds social proof and improves local search rankings.
Trade-in valuation
AI-powered trade-in valuation tool on your website. Customers enter their vehicle details and receive an instant, accurate estimate. Captures lead information and triggers immediate follow-up from the sales team with a firm offer and appointment link.
Service appointment AI
AI-powered service scheduling that integrates with your shop capacity and parts inventory. Automated service reminders, recall notifications, and multi-point inspection follow-ups. Keeps the service bay full and drives dealership loyalty.
Financing automation
Automated credit application processing and financing workflow. Customers submit applications online, AI pre-qualifies based on credit criteria, and routes to the appropriate lender. Reduces F&I processing time and speeds up the sales cycle.
Social media ads
AI-driven social media advertising for Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. Dynamic vehicle listing ads, retargeting campaigns, and lookalike audience targeting. Real-time performance optimization based on cost-per-lead and cost-per-sale by model and segment.
