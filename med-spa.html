Why med spas struggle to grow.

Most med spas leave revenue on the table because their client acquisition and retention run on manual, fragmented systems. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Consultation booking friction

Potential clients fill out web forms, send DMs, and call — but without automated booking, consultations get delayed or lost entirely. Every hour of delay means a higher chance they book with a competitor. Our AI captures and schedules instantly.

Follow-up leakage

Clients who don't purchase on the first visit, don't rebook within the optimal window, or haven't been seen in months — these are your biggest revenue leaks. Without automated follow-up sequences, you're leaving 40%+ of potential revenue on the table.

Membership retention

Monthly membership programs are the backbone of med spa recurring revenue, but manual tracking and renewal reminders lead to churn. Most med spas lose 25-30% of members annually simply because they forget to re-engage before cancellation.

Competitive ad costs

Med spa advertising costs have skyrocketed as more clinics compete for the same Botox, filler, and laser clients. Without AI-driven campaign optimization and audience targeting, you're overpaying for every click while competitors capture your ideal clients.
