Why info product creators struggle to grow.

Most creators leave revenue on the table because their student engagement and sales systems run on manual, fragmented processes. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Student engagement drops

Course completion rates average below 20% across most platforms. Students buy, start strong, then fade. Without automated engagement sequences, you're leaving future upsells, referrals, and reviews on the table — along with your students' outcomes.

Cart abandonment

70% of info product carts are abandoned before purchase. Most creators have no automated recovery system — no follow-up emails, no retargeting, no abandoned checkout SMS. Every abandoned cart is a direct revenue loss that could be recovered with AI.

Community management overload

Running a thriving student community requires 24/7 moderation, engagement, and support. Without AI assistance, you're either neglecting your community or burning out trying to keep up. Both scenarios lead to churn and bad word-of-mouth.

Upsell timing is off

Most creators leave money on the table because they have no systematic way to identify the right moment to offer a upsell, bundle, or higher-tier product. Without AI-driven behavioral triggers, upsells feel pushy or get sent too late — or never at all.
