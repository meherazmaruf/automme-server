Building high-performance AI.

Maruf started working with early AI models to automate complex workflows and drive business efficiency. His focus on practical, ROI-driven automation led to the founding of Automme. What began as a solo consultancy quickly grew into a full-service AI systems firm serving clients across North America, Europe, and Asia.

From there, he began experimenting with advanced LLMs to automate content production and lead generation, proving the financial utility of generative AI for B2B companies. He published his findings openly, building an audience of thousands who follow his work. His frameworks for AI-driven content operations have been adopted by agencies generating eight-figure revenues.

He's since launched and scaled Automme to help businesses achieve scalable growth, building systems that are relied on by clients worldwide. Today, Automme designs, deploys, and maintains AI infrastructure that runs mission-critical operations across dozens of industries. Every system is built for performance, reliability, and real business impact — not just proof of concepts.
