Why dermatology & skincare practices struggle to grow.

Most dermatology and skincare practices lose patients to slow lead response, poor follow-up, and inconsistent patient communication. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Lead response delay

Patients researching skin concerns book the practice that responds first. Without AI that answers website chats, calls, and forms instantly, you lose potential patients to competitors who respond in seconds instead of hours or days.

Consultation no-shows

New patient consultations for aesthetic treatments have high no-show rates — people book impulsively and forget or change their minds. Without automated confirmation, reminder, and re-engagement sequences, every empty consultation slot is lost revenue.

Treatment follow-up gaps

Patients need follow-up after treatments — check results, schedule next sessions, maintain skincare routines. Without automated post-treatment sequences, patients don't return for recommended follow-ups and results suffer, along with your reputation.

Product & service upsell leakage

Every patient visit is an opportunity to recommend medical-grade skincare products or additional treatments. Without AI-driven product recommendations and upsell sequences, you leave thousands in revenue on the table per patient per year.
