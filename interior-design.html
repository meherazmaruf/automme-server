Why interior design firms struggle to scale.

Most design firms lose revenue to inconsistent lead flow, project management chaos, and manual vendor coordination. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Inconsistent lead flow

Interior design is project-based — when projects end, leads must fill the pipeline. Without automated lead generation and nurturing, you cycle between feast and famine. Our AI keeps your pipeline full with qualified residential and commercial prospects.

Project management chaos

Orders, deliveries, installs, client approvals, change orders — managing design projects manually is overwhelming. Without AI project coordination, deadlines slip, budgets blow, and client satisfaction suffers.

Vendor & procurement bottlenecks

Ordering from dozens of vendors across trade programs, tracking lead times, and managing freight is a full-time job. Without AI procurement automation, your team spends more time chasing orders than designing.

Proposal & billing delays

Creating professional proposals, collecting deposits, and managing milestone billing takes hours per project. Without AI proposal generation and billing automation, you delay project starts and slow cash flow.
