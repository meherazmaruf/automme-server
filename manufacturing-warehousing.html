Why manufacturers & warehouse operators struggle to scale.

Most manufacturing and warehousing operations lose millions to downtime, inventory waste, manual quality checks, and supply chain blind spots. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Unplanned downtime

Equipment failures halt production lines costing $10K+ per minute in lost output. Without predictive maintenance AI, you're fixing machines after they break instead of preventing breakdowns before they happen. Our AI predicts failures 48-72 hours in advance.

Inventory blind spots

Excess stock ties up capital while stockouts halt production. Without real-time inventory forecasting, you're guessing what to order and when. Our AI analyzes consumption patterns, lead times, and seasonal demand to optimize every SKU.

Quality control bottlenecks

Manual inspection is slow, inconsistent, and misses defects. Without AI-powered visual inspection and real-time quality monitoring, defective products reach customers, returns pile up, and brand reputation suffers. Our AI catches defects human eyes miss.

Supply chain fragmentation

Supplier delays, logistics disruptions, and demand fluctuations create chaos. Without a centralized AI that monitors every link in your supply chain, you're always reacting to problems instead of anticipating them. Our AI provides end-to-end visibility and automated mitigation.

Everything your manufacturing & warehousing operation needs.

A complete AI-powered operations stack for manufacturing and warehousing. From raw material to finished goods, every system works together to maximize throughput and minimize waste.

Predictive maintenance AI
AI that monitors equipment vibration, temperature, power draw, and run hours to predict failures before they happen. Automated maintenance scheduling, parts ordering, and technician dispatch. Reduce unplanned downtime by up to 60% and extend equipment life.
Inventory forecasting
AI-powered demand forecasting that analyzes historical consumption, seasonality, supplier lead times, and market trends. Automated reorder points, safety stock calculations, and purchase order generation. Reduce carrying costs while eliminating stockouts.
Quality control vision AI
Computer vision systems that inspect every product at line speed — detecting dimensional defects, surface flaws, color variations, and assembly errors. Real-time alerts, automated rejection, and continuous quality scoring. Catch defects humans cannot see.
Supply chain control tower
Centralized AI that monitors every link in your supply chain — supplier performance, logistics, inventory levels, and demand signals. Real-time disruption alerts, automated mitigation workflows, and supplier scorecards. End-to-end visibility from raw materials to delivery.
Production monitoring dashboards
Real-time OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness) dashboards showing availability, performance, and quality metrics per line, shift, and product. AI identifies bottleneck stations, optimal changeover schedules, and throughput improvement opportunities.
Warehouse optimization AI
AI-driven slotting optimization, pick path routing, labor forecasting, and throughput analytics. Automated put-away recommendations, dynamic wave planning, and real-time congestion detection. Maximize cubic utilization and minimize travel time.
Supplier performance management
Automated supplier scorecards tracking on-time delivery, quality rates, lead time variability, and pricing trends. AI identifies at-risk suppliers and suggests alternate sourcing before disruptions occur. Quarterly business reviews generated automatically.
Labor productivity analytics
AI tracks labor utilization across shifts, lines, and roles. Identifies training gaps, optimal crew sizes, and cross-training opportunities. Automated time tracking, productivity scoring, and incentive calculation. Reduce labor cost per unit produced.
Energy management AI
AI-powered energy monitoring that identifies waste, optimizes machine scheduling around peak rates, and automates HVAC and lighting in warehouse zones. Real-time consumption dashboards with anomaly detection. Cut energy costs by 15-30% without production impact.
Demand sensing & planning
AI that analyzes customer orders, market trends, economic indicators, and even weather data to predict demand with 85%+ accuracy. Automated production scheduling, raw material procurement triggers, and capacity planning aligned to forecasted demand.
Compliance & safety automation
AI-powered safety monitoring using computer vision to detect PPE violations, unsafe behaviors, and hazard zones. Automated OSHA reporting, incident documentation, and safety training assignments. Reduce workplace incidents and compliance overhead.
