Why roofing contractors leave money on the table.

Most roofing companies lose revenue because their lead capture and operations run on manual, reactive systems. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Storm leads slip away

After a hailstorm, every roofing contractor in town competes for the same leads. Without automated storm detection and instant outreach, you're days behind your competitors. Our AI monitors weather data and triggers lead capture campaigns within hours of a storm event.

Insurance claim delays

Homeowners delay filing insurance claims, and without a system to guide them through the process, jobs stall or die. Our automated claim follow-up sequences keep homeowners moving through the adjuster process and into your production pipeline.

Seasonal revenue gaps

Roofing is seasonal. Storm season brings a flood of work, then dry spells follow. Without intelligent marketing automation, you either scramble to handle the surge or struggle to find work in the off-season. Our AI smooths revenue year-round.

Inconsistent estimates

Every estimator prices jobs differently, leading to inconsistent margins and lost bids. Without standardized estimate automation, you're leaving money on the table or pricing yourself out of jobs. Our AI generates consistent, accurate quotes in seconds.

Storm lead capture

Insurance claim follow-up
Automated sequences that guide homeowners through the insurance claim process. Step-by-step SMS and email campaigns with adjuster appointment reminders, document requests, and status updates. Keep claims moving and jobs on track.
Review generation
Automated review request campaigns that generate a steady stream of Google and Facebook reviews. Smart routing sends happy homeowners to review platforms while routing complaints to a private channel. Boost your local ranking and win more bids.
Google Maps lead gen
AI-powered local lead extraction from Google Maps, property records, and insurance databases. Identify homeowners with aging roofs, recent storm activity, or expired insurance claims. Automated outreach turns data into booked inspections.
CRM automation
Every lead — call, form fill, storm alert, social DM — automatically enters your CRM, gets scored by value and urgency, assigned to the right salesperson, and triggered into a personalized follow-up sequence. No manual data entry.

Automated drone inspection report processing. AI analyzes aerial imagery to identify roof damage, measure square footage, and generate professional inspection reports. Integrates directly with your estimating and CRM systems.
Referral systems
