Why commercial cleaning companies struggle to scale.

Most cleaning companies hit a revenue ceiling because their operations, sales, and retention run on manual processes. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Route inefficiency

Cleaning crews drive between accounts without optimized routing, burning fuel and billable hours. Without AI route optimization, you lose 15-25% of productive time to unnecessary travel, and your margins shrink with every new account at scale.

Client churn

Commercial cleaning contracts churn because clients feel ignored between sales calls. Without automated check-ins, satisfaction surveys, and renewal campaigns at 90, 60, and 30 days, you lose accounts to competitors who stay top-of-mind.

Bidding burnout

Every RFP and bid proposal takes hours of manual work — measuring square footage, calculating labor, pricing supplies. Without AI-powered bid generation, your team spends more time writing proposals than cleaning, and you win fewer bids.

Labor scheduling hell

Call-outs, schedule changes, and client scope changes create daily chaos for dispatchers. Without AI workforce management, you overstaff some accounts, understaff others, and burn out your best employees with unpredictable schedules.

Workforce scheduling
AI that builds optimal crew schedules based on account locations, service hours, employee skills, and availability. Automated shift swapping, call-out coverage, and time tracking. Reduce scheduling overhead by 80% while improving employee satisfaction.
Supply inventory management
AI tracks cleaning supply usage per account, predicts reorder points, and automates purchase orders. Never run out of paper towels or all-purpose cleaner mid-week. Reduce supply costs by eliminating waste and emergency purchases.
Quality assurance AI
Digital QA checklists with photo verification, automated inspections, and client satisfaction scoring. AI analyzes inspection data to identify training gaps, high-risk accounts, and improvement opportunities. Prove quality to clients with data, not promises.

Automated contract management — renewal reminders, price escalation based on contracted terms, and scope change proposals. AI identifies accounts ready for upsell (add windows, carpets, construction cleanup) and triggers targeted campaigns.
