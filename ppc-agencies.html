Why PPC agencies struggle to scale.

Most PPC agencies are trapped in a cycle of manual optimization that limits how many accounts they can profitably manage. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Bid management overhead

Manual bid adjustments across hundreds of keywords, multiple platforms, and dozens of accounts consume hours every day. Most agencies can't afford the headcount to optimize properly, so they leave money on the table. Our AI handles bid optimization at machine speed, 24/7.

Ad fatigue

Running the same creative for weeks kills CTR and drives up costs. But generating, testing, and rotating ad copy at scale is brutally manual. Without AI, most agencies rotate creative far too slowly and watch performance decay.

Cross-platform complexity

Managing campaigns across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok, Amazon, and more means logging into 6+ dashboards daily. Cross-platform reporting is a spreadsheet nightmare. Our unified platform lets you manage and report from a single pane of glass.

Reporting burden

Building client reports eats 10-20 hours per week per account manager. Every report requires pulling data from multiple sources, formatting charts, and writing commentary. It's the most hated part of the job — and the most automatable.
