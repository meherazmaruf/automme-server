Why multi-channel marketing is broken.

Most businesses waste 50%+ of their marketing budget because their channels operate in silos with no unified strategy or AI-driven optimization. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Channel fragmentation

Your audience is spread across Google, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, email, and more — but each channel has its own team, tools, and strategy. Without unified AI orchestration, you're running seven separate campaigns instead of one cohesive system.

Creative fatigue kills performance

Ad fatigue sets in fast — audiences get bored, CTR drops, and costs rise. Without automated creative testing and rotation, your campaigns degrade over time. Most businesses don't test enough creatives, fast enough, to maintain performance.

Attribution complexity

Customers interact with 5-7 touchpoints before converting — but most businesses use last-click attribution. You don't know which channels drive real results, so you under-invest in what works and over-spend on what doesn't. AI solves the attribution puzzle.

Budget optimization is guesswork

Without AI-driven budget allocation, you're making gut-feel decisions about where to spend next dollar. Market conditions change hourly — competitor moves, algorithm updates, seasonal shifts. Manual optimization can't keep up with real-time market dynamics.
