Why SaaS companies leak growth.

Even world-class products struggle with user activation, retention, and support at scale. Here are the most common growth leaks we fix for SaaS companies.

User onboarding that doesn't convert

Most SaaS products lose 40-60% of new users in the first week. Confusing onboarding flows, unclear value prop, and delayed time-to-value drive users away before they experience your core product. AI personalizes onboarding in real-time based on user behavior.

Churn you can't predict or prevent

By the time you notice a user is at risk, they're already gone. Manual churn analysis is reactive and slow. Our AI models identify at-risk users 30-60 days before churn, triggering automated interventions that have cut churn by 35%+ for our clients.

Support team drowning in tickets

As you scale users, support tickets scale exponentially. Tier-1 questions about billing, account setup, and basic features consume hours that your team should spend on complex customer problems. AI support automation handles 60-80% of tickets without human touch.

Feature adoption stuck below 20%

You ship amazing features, but nobody uses them. Without intelligent feature discovery and guided adoption, users stick to the 20% they know. Our AI drives feature adoption through contextual prompts, personalized training, and usage-based triggers.
