Why logistics & freight brokerages struggle to scale.

Most brokerages lose margin to manual load matching, slow carrier sourcing, and rate negotiation inefficiency. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Manual load matching

Brokers spend hours on load boards searching for the right carrier. Without AI load matching, your team covers the same lanes inefficiently and margin gets left on the table. Our AI matches loads to the best carrier in seconds.

Carrier sourcing bottlenecks

Finding and qualifying new carriers is manual and slow. Without automated carrier sourcing and vetting, you rely on the same limited carrier pool and miss capacity opportunities. Our AI sources and qualifies carriers at scale.

Rate negotiation leakage

Brokers lose margin because they lack real-time market rate data during negotiations. Without AI-powered rate analytics and recommendations, you're negotiating blind and leaving money on every load.

Client churn & growth

Shippers switch brokers when communication breaks down. Without automated client check-ins, performance reporting, and proactive outreach, you lose accounts to competitors who provide better visibility and service.

Load matching AI
AI that scans DAT, Truckstop, and 123Loadboard to find the best carrier for every load based on lane, equipment type, capacity, and rate history. Real-time matching with push notifications. Reduce load coverage time by 80%.
Carrier sourcing & vetting
Automated carrier discovery that finds and qualifies new carriers based on authority, insurance, safety rating, and lane coverage. AI-powered vetting with automated document collection and compliance checks. Build a bigger carrier network without manual effort.
Rate negotiation AI
Real-time market rate intelligence based on lane, equipment type, season, and demand. AI recommends optimal rates, flags below-margin loads, and provides data-backed negotiation talking points. Increase margin per load by 15-25%.
Dispatch optimization
AI that assigns loads to the right dispatcher or broker based on lane expertise, workload, and client relationship. Automated load tendering, tracking updates, and delivery confirmation. Reduce dispatch overhead and improve service levels.
Client CRM & retention
Automated shipper relationship management — load status updates, weekly performance reports, quarterly business reviews, and proactive check-ins. AI identifies at-risk accounts and triggers retention sequences with personalized service offers.
Customer load booking AI
Conversational AI that handles shipper inquiries, captures load details, provides instant rate quotes, and books loads automatically. 24/7 load booking via phone, email, chat, and portal. Never miss a load opportunity from a shipper.
TMS integration & automation
AI that integrates with your existing TMS to automate data entry, invoice generation, settlement processing, and document management. Eliminate duplicate data entry across load boards, TMS, and accounting. Reduce administrative overhead by 60%.
Broker productivity analytics
AI tracks every broker's performance — loads covered, margin per load, carrier relationships, client retention, and revenue per week. Identifies top performers and their winning patterns. Automated coaching for underperformers.
Carrier portal & self-service
White-label carrier portal where carriers find available loads, book directly, upload documents, track payments, and communicate with dispatchers. AI recommends loads based on carrier preferences and performance history.
Sales & shipper acquisition

Factoring & payments automation
