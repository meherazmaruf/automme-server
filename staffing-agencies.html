Why staffing agencies struggle to scale.

Most staffing agencies lose revenue to manual resume screening, slow client outreach, and candidate drop-off. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Resume screening overload

Recruiters spend 70% of their time screening resumes that don't match. Without AI screening, your best talent sourcers are buried in irrelevant applications instead of building relationships with top candidates.

Client acquisition slowdown

Winning new staffing contracts requires consistent outreach to hiring managers, HR directors, and procurement teams. Without automated client prospecting, your desk stays quiet and your best recruiters waste time on manual cold outreach.

Candidate ghosting

Candidates go dark after interviews, offers get declined, and placements fall through. Without automated candidate engagement sequences, you lose top talent to agencies who stay more connected throughout the hiring process.

Placement churn

Temp placements walk off the job, contract-to-hire conversions fail, and clients lose confidence. Without automated check-ins and satisfaction monitoring, you don't know a placement is failing until it's too late to save it.

Resume screening automation

Interview scheduling AI
Conversational AI that coordinates interview scheduling between candidates, clients, and your team. Automated calendar sync, time zone detection, reminder sequences, and rescheduling. Reduce scheduling back-and-forth from days to minutes.

Placement follow-up AI
Automated check-in sequences for placed candidates — day 1, week 1, month 1, month 3. AI surveys both candidate and client satisfaction, flags at-risk placements, and triggers intervention workflows. Improve fill rates and contract extensions.
