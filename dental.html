Why dental practices struggle to grow.

Most dental practices leave money on the table because their patient acquisition and operations run on outdated systems. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Missed calls = missed patients

Dental practices lose up to 40% of new patient opportunities because phones go unanswered after hours or during busy periods. Every missed call is a lifetime patient value walking out the door. Our AI answers, qualifies, and books — 24/7, in any language.

PPC spend with zero visibility

Most dental ad campaigns run blind — no idea which keywords drive actual booked appointments versus wasted clicks. We connect your Google Ads to your practice management software so every dollar is tracked to a confirmed chair.

Leads fall through the cracks

Web form submissions, Facebook messages, Google reviews — leads come from everywhere but land nowhere. Without automated routing, they sit in an inbox until they go cold. Our CRM automation assigns, alerts, and follows up instantly.

No-show rates killing production

Dental practices battle 15-25% no-show rates that destroy daily production targets. Manual reminder calls don't scale. Our multi-channel appointment reminder system (SMS, email, voice) cuts no-shows to under 5%.
