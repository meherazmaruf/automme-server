Why wholesale & distribution companies struggle to scale.

Most distributors lose millions to excess inventory, stockouts, manual order processing, and supplier inefficiency. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Inventory imbalance

Excess stock ties up capital while stockouts lose sales. Without AI-powered demand forecasting, you're guessing what to buy and when. Our AI analyzes historical sales, seasonality, trends, and supplier lead times to optimize every SKU.

Manual order processing

Orders come in via phone, email, EDI, and portal — all requiring manual entry into your ERP. Without AI order capture and processing, your team spends hours on data entry instead of sales and relationship building.

Supplier fragmentation

Managing dozens or hundreds of suppliers across different portals, price lists, and lead times creates chaos. Without AI supplier management, you miss pricing changes, delay purchase orders, and lose margin.

Customer retention leakage

Wholesale customers churn when they feel underserved between orders. Without automated reorder reminders, personalized recommendations, and proactive outreach, your best accounts get poached by competitors.
