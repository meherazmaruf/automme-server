Why IT support companies struggle to scale.

Most IT support and computer repair businesses lose revenue to ticket backlog, inefficient dispatch, and reactive service models. Here are the most common breakdowns we fix.

Ticket triage bottleneck

Every support request comes through a different channel — email, chat, phone, portal — and your team spends hours just sorting and prioritizing. Without AI ticket triage, urgent issues get buried under low-priority requests and SLAs get missed.

Reactive service trap

You only make money when things break. Without proactive monitoring and automated maintenance campaigns, you're stuck in a break-fix cycle with unpredictable revenue and no recurring income to build on.

Technician utilization

On-site techs waste hours driving between jobs, and remote techs juggle too many tickets at once. Without AI dispatch and scheduling, your most expensive resource — technician time — is constantly underutilized or misallocated.

Client churn

IT clients switch providers because they feel unsupported between tickets. Without automated check-ins, quarterly business reviews, and proactive system health reporting, you lose accounts to competitors who communicate better.
